Who We Are

A Nurturing Space
Built for Families

The Conscious Family Centre (CFC) is a nature-connected alternative education and family playgroup space in Abuja — designed for children ages 0 to 10. We offer stay-and-play sessions, a homeschool hub, and community support for parents and caregivers.

🌳

Nature-Connected Education

Forest school, outdoor play and earth-based learning that nurtures curiosity naturally.

👨‍👩‍👧

Community for Families

A warm, supportive space for parents, caregivers and children to grow together.

🎓

Professional Nanny Training

Intensive caregiver development programme equipping nannies with modern childcare skills.

Why Choose CFC

A Space That Truly Cares

🌿

Nature-Led Approach

We use forest school philosophy and outdoor learning as the foundation of all our activities — rooted in the rhythms of nature.

Safe & Inclusive Space
