Our Story

A nature-connected haven for children 0–10 and their families

Conscious Family Centre (CFC)

We are a nature-connected alternative education and family playgroup space in Abuja, designed for children ages 0 to 10. Our centre offers stay-and-play sessions, a Homeschool Hub, and dedicated community support for parents and caregivers.

We believe that childhood should be filled with wonder, outdoor exploration, and meaningful connections. Every activity is intentionally crafted to nurture emotional, social, and cognitive development.
