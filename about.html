Our Story

Born from a Belief in Conscious Childhood

The Conscious Family Centre was founded on a simple but powerful belief — that every child deserves a childhood rich in nature, play, creativity and genuine human connection.

Nestled inside the lush BMT Garden in Wuse 2, Abuja, CFC was created as a response to families seeking something more intentional for their young children. A space that honours the whole child — mind, body, and spirit — and brings parents and caregivers into the journey too.

From forest school sessions under open skies to structured homeschool hubs and professional nanny training, we've built a layered ecosystem of learning and support for families across Abuja.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Values

Every programme, every session, every interaction at CFC flows from these core commitments.

🌱

Intentional Growth

We believe growth is cultivated, not rushed. We create conditions where children naturally blossom at their own rhythm and pace.

🤝

Community & Belonging

No family should feel alone on this journey. CFC is a village — a place where parents, nannies and children all find their people.

🌍

Rooted in Nature

Nature is our greatest teacher. We use the outdoors, seasons, and elements of the earth as the core of our curriculum.

✨

Wonder & Curiosity

We protect the magic of childhood. Every session is designed to spark curiosity, delight and the joy of discovery.

🛡️

Safety & Trust

Parents trust us with their most precious people. We hold that trust with the highest standards of care, structure and warmth.

🎯

Professional Excellence

From our educators to our trained nannies, we hold ourselves to the highest standards of professional childcare practice.
