Before You Visit

Good Things to Know

A few helpful notes to make your first visit smooth and enjoyable.

📞 We recommend calling ahead for first-time visits to confirm session availability and get directions to our BMT Garden location.
👕 Dress children in comfortable, play-ready clothing. Outdoor and messy play sessions happen regularly!
💡 For Nanny Intensive Training enrolment, please call us to discuss scheduling and intake dates.
🚌 Waka Wednesday excursions happen weekly. Contact us to ensure your child doesn't miss the adventure!

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the things families ask us most often.

🌿 What age groups do you accept?

CFC welcomes children from birth (0 years) up to 10 years old. We have activities and sessions designed specifically for each developmental stage.

📅 Do I need to book in advance?

For Stay & Play sessions, drop-ins are welcome. However, we recommend calling ahead, especially for first visits and programme-specific sessions like Nanny Training.

👩‍🏫 Who can enrol in Nanny Training?

The Nanny Intensive Training programme is open to nannies, caregivers, and anyone interested in professional childcare. Contact us to discuss the next intake date.

🏕️ Can parents stay during sessions?

Yes! Our Stay & Play sessions are specifically designed for parent-accompanied play. Parents are not just welcome — they're part of the experience.

🔬 When do Holiday Camps run?

Holiday Camps run during school break periods. Follow us on Instagram @consciousfamilycentre or call us to find out about upcoming camp dates and themes.

🚌 What is Waka Wednesday?

Waka Wednesday is our weekly excursion programme where children explore Abuja — parks, museums, markets and community spaces. It runs every Wednesday during term.
