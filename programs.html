Our Programs & Activities

Immersive learning experiences designed for joyful childhood discovery

Core Programs

We offer comprehensive programs that combine structured learning with unstructured play, community connection, and nature immersion.

Weekly

Stay & Play Sessions

Flexible, drop-in sessions where families come together for child-led play, social development, and milestone celebrations. Perfect for children ages 0-5 to explore, connect, and grow at their own pace.

Full-Time

Homeschool Hub & Community

A dedicated learning space for homeschooling families. Access to library resources, enrichment activities, peer learning opportunities, and parental support. Flexible schedules to suit your family's rhythm.

Professional

Nanny Intensive Training

Specialized vocational training for nannies and caregivers. Learn sensory care, child psychology, creative facilitation, safety protocols, and conscious parenting approaches to elevate your professional practice.

Seasonal

Holiday Camps & STEAM

Immersive exploration during school breaks. Scavenger hunts, messy play, science experiments, hands-on building, nature tracking, and transport physics. Perfect for curious minds ages 5-10.

Weekly Core Activities

These regular activities form the heart of our program, offering children consistent, engaging experiences throughout the week.

Outdoor

Forest School & Outdoor Play

Raw exploration of natural terrain. Children navigate woods, discover ecosystems, track wildlife, build shelters, and develop confidence and resilience through unstructured outdoor play.

Creative

Creative Arts & Crafts

Focus on textures, canvas, and fine motor development. Children paint, sculpt, design, and create without pressure. Art becomes a language for expression and exploration.

Enrichment

Weekly Enrichment Clubs

Rotating themed clubs including music, dance, storytelling, nature studies, and sensory exploration. Each week brings new discovery and skill-building in a play-based environment.

Adventure

Waka Wednesday Excursions

Weekly adventures exploring community spaces, cultural sites, museums, and natural landmarks. Children learn about their environment and culture through real-world exploration.
