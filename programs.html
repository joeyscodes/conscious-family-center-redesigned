Our Programs & Activities

Purposefully designed experiences that nurture the whole child — through play, nature, creativity and community connection.

Stay & Play

Program 01

Stay & Play Sessions

Our signature Stay & Play sessions are drop-in sessions where parents and caregivers stay alongside their children in a stimulating, supervised, and nurturing environment. Designed for children ages 0–10, these sessions blend free play with guided activities in a warm community setting.

Children explore, create, move and connect — while parents build friendships and receive support from our team of trained facilitators.

Open to children ages 0–10
Free play meets structured exploration
Parent-accompanied sessions
Monday – Saturday, 10AM – 3PM
Supportive, trained facilitators on site

Homeschool Hub

Program 02

Homeschool Hub & Community

The CFC Homeschool Hub is a structured yet flexible learning space designed to support families who have chosen the homeschool path. We provide a community environment where homeschooled children benefit from peer interaction, enrichment activities, and access to our knowledgeable facilitators.

Beyond academics, we focus on socialisation, creative development, and the kind of whole-child growth that homeschool families value most.

Structured learning support
Peer socialisation opportunities
Weekly enrichment activities
Community of homeschool families
Resource sharing & parent support

Nanny Training

Program 03

Nanny Intensive Training

Our Nanny Intensive Training programme is a professional development course designed to equip nannies and caregivers in Abuja with the knowledge, skills and confidence to provide high-quality childcare. Covering child development, safety, creative play, and professional ethics.

Graduates leave with a certificate of completion and a community of fellow trained professionals — elevating the standard of childcare across Abuja one caregiver at a time.

Child development foundations
Safety, first aid & emergency protocols
Creative play & activity facilitation
Professional ethics & communication
Certificate of completion awarded

Holiday Camps & STEAM

Program 04

Holiday Camps & STEAM

Our Holiday Camps bring the adventure of learning to life during school breaks. Packed with hands-on STEAM activities — Science experiments, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Maths — alongside scavenger hunts and legendary messy play sessions that children absolutely love.

Each camp is themed and carefully structured to be both educational and wildly fun — the kind of holiday memories children carry for life.

STEAM-based experiments & projects
Nature scavenger hunts
Messy play & sensory exploration
Themed daily adventures
School holiday scheduling

Every Week at CFC

Core Activities

These are the beating heart of everything we do — weekly experiences that spark curiosity, build confidence and create lasting memories.

🌳
🌳

Forest School & Outdoor Play

Earth-based learning in the open air. Children explore nature, build dens, observe wildlife and develop a deep, meaningful connection to the natural world through forest school methodology.

🎨
🎨

Creative Arts & Crafts

Painting, sculpting, collaging and messy play — every week brings a new creative adventure. These sessions develop fine motor skills, self-expression and the pure joy of making something with your hands.

🧩
🧩

Weekly Enrichment Clubs

From chess to storytelling, music to coding-for-kids — our weekly clubs give children the chance to go deep into areas of interest, develop skills and connect with other children who share their passions.

🚌
🚌

Waka Wednesday Excursions

Every Wednesday, we go on an adventure! Waka Wednesday takes children on guided excursions around Abuja — to parks, markets, museums and community spaces. The world beyond the classroom becomes the curriculum.
